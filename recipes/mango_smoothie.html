Mango Smoothie

Description

A cold, creamy mango smoothie is the best summertime smoothie recipe. And it only takes three minutes to whip up!

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh mangoes
  • 1 small frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup yogurt

Steps

  1. Get all ingredients together
  2. Blend until creamy!
